How Disney+ TV Show Ratings Have Changed Over Time

The visualization above explores how Disney TV show age ratings have changed throughout different five-year intervals. Use the slider above to examine how the distribution of ratings change over time. This chart helps reveal production trends in whether Disney has produced more content for younger audiences or aimed toward older viewers. The following age ratings include TV-Y, TV-Y7, TV-Y7-FV, TV-G, TV-PG, and TV-14.
